Effective Date: August 6, 2025

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To deliver gang sheet generation services
  • To improve site performance and user experience
  • To communicate with you about updates or support
  • To process payments via Stripe

3. Your Rights

You can contact us at info@ezgangsheets.com to update or delete your account.

4. Security

We take steps to protect your information, but no system is 100% secure. Please use EZGangSheets at your own discretion.
